## Project Overview

**Manzarri** is a robust, full-stack e-commerce platform specializing in the **jewellery market**. The project was conceived and developed during an intensive **seven-day hackathon**, with the core objective of creating a deployable application supported by a solid business model. Leveraging a background in family retail, the developer identified a need to build a high-quality online storefront that could compete in a differentiated niche.

The initial concept was a clothing store, but recognizing high market saturation among other hackathon participants (approx. 70%), a strategic pivot was executed to focus on the **gold and fine jewellery market**. This decision prioritized **business coherence** and competitive differentiation.

## Enhanced Features & Technical Implementation

Beyond the core requirements, Manzarri was developed with advanced features typically found in established e-commerce businesses:

* **Secure Payments:** Integration with **Stripe (test environment)** for reliable transaction processing.
* **Logistics Management:** Simple **shipment API integration** to streamline fulfillment.
* **Administration:** A dedicated **Admin Dashboard** for comprehensive store management.
* **Analytics:** **Google Analytics setup** for tracking user behavior and sales performance.
* **Order Placement Cycle:** Developed using **TypeScript with Object-Oriented Programming (OOP) principles**, ensuring the cycle is super **robust, scalable, and highly maintainable**.
* **Multi-Tenant Authentication:** A secure and robust authentication system defining separate roles for **Users** and **Administrators**.

## Future Roadmap & Innovation

The project is under continuous development with a strong focus on **automation and cutting-edge technology** to create a highly autonomous and profitable business:

* **Autonomous Store Management:** Implementation of **AI Agents** designed to autonomously manage store operations (e.g., product updates, pricing strategies).
* **Advanced Customer & Lead Generation:** Development of autonomous agents specializing in **lead generation** and providing **customer care and support**.
* **Inventory Management System (IMS):** Converting the existing product collection into a complete IMS with the functionality to use AI/image processing to **generate unique identification labels or QR codes** for physical products.
* **Customization Engine:** Adding a feature to allow customers to **order custom jewellery**.
* **Transparency Feature:** Implementing a **Guest Functionality** on the dashboard, offering curious users and stakeholders a guided tour of the operational backend with strictly isolated, view-only privileges.

**Manzarri** represents a strong foundation for a scalable e-commerce business, combining rapid development with a strategic market niche and a clear, innovative roadmap toward autonomy and growth.
